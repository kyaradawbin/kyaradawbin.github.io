Frequently Asked Questions.

How many languages do you speak?

I speak English fluently and Spanish at an intermediate level (B1). However, I can read a newspaper article in Romance languages and I have a very basic understanding of Dutch, Swedish, and German. Because the definition of "fluency" is so vague, I refer to myself as an "ex-monoglot"--or a person who no longer speaks just one language.

What languages are you learning?

Because I encounter a lot of different languages on a regular basis, I pick up words here and there. I am serious about learning Spanish, French, and German. In the future, I would like to be conversationally fluent in the official languages of the United Nations.

What is your dream career?

I would love to be a computational linguist, which pretty much means that I combine two of my interests into a really futuristic-sounding job title.

What are your hobbies?

Art is fascinating to me and I find myself doodling a lot in my spare time. I also like to garden, go on hikes, and make up fantasy worlds (with constructed lands, languages, and histories). Overall, I have a lot of different interests and I tend to bounce between them.
